CyberSafe

Navigate the Internet Safely

A board game experience teaching online safety through real-life Australian cyber scenarios. Roll the dice, face challenges, and make smart choices to reach the end safely!

🎮 Play Game 📚 Learn Mode

How to Play

🎲

Roll & Move

Roll the dice and navigate your way through the 10×10 internet board.

🔎

Face Challenges

Land on action squares to encounter cyber scenarios you need to respond to.

🏁

Reach the End

Make smart choices to keep your lives and reach the finish line safely.

Cyber Threats You'll Face

🎓 Student Threats

Phishing emails, credential theft, academic fraud, and ransomware targeting educational data.

💼 Office Threats

Business email compromise, password security, public Wi-Fi risks, and insider threats.

🏠 Home Threats

Online shopping scams, smart device vulnerabilities, identity theft, and social media fraud.

Created for Challenge 3 — Vibe Coding: Play to Impact
