Our team combines expertise in game design, web development, and cyber security to create an innovative learning experience that addresses real threats facing Australian communities.

Project Background

Challenge 3: Vibe Coding - Play to Impact

CyberSafe was created as our submission for Challenge 3 of Vibe Coding, which challenged us to combine game design with meaningful real-world impact.

We identified a critical gap in cyber security education and designed an engaging board game experience that makes learning about digital safety fun and practical.

Focusing on Australian Threats

Our content is based on real cyber threats facing Australians in three key environments: educational institutions, office workplaces, and home networks.

Drawing from the Australian Signals Directorate's 2024-25 Annual Cyber Threat Report, we created 60 realistic scenarios that educate while entertaining.
